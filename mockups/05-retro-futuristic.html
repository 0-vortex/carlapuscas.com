What I offer

Three areas of expertise

Clinical Psychology

One-on-one therapy and psychological assessment for adults working through anxiety, depression, transitions, and the challenges that shape a life. Evidence-based, relationship-centered.

Educational Psychology

Assessment and support for learners navigating differences, giftedness, and developmental questions. Helping children, adolescents, and families understand cognitive and emotional strengths.

Vocational Counseling

Structured career guidance through validated assessments and reflective conversation. For anyone seeking clarity about their professional direction and the work that matters to them.
