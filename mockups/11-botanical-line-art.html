Areas of Practice

Clinical Psychology

Compassionate, evidence-based therapy for adults experiencing anxiety, depression, grief, and major life transitions. Together we cultivate the inner resources needed for lasting change.

Educational Psychology

Thorough assessments for children and adolescents to understand learning differences, attention, and emotional needs. Helping families and schools create the right conditions for every learner to thrive.

Guided career exploration and assessment for people at meaningful crossroads. Clarifying your strengths, values, and aspirations to find professional paths that truly fit your life.
