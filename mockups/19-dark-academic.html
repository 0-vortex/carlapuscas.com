Areas of Practice

Three Pillars of Support

I

Clinical Psychology

Therapeutic work with adults facing anxiety, mood disorders, trauma, grief, and the persistent questions that surface during life's most demanding chapters. Grounded in evidence-based practice with an emphasis on the therapeutic relationship.

II

Educational Psychology

Comprehensive psychoeducational assessment for learners of all ages. Understanding cognitive profiles, learning differences, giftedness, and the interplay between emotional wellbeing and academic achievement.

III

Vocational Counseling

Structured guidance for those at professional crossroads. Through validated assessment tools and reflective conversation, we explore your aptitudes, values, and the work that would bring genuine fulfillment.
