Three Pillars of Practice

I

Clinical Psychology

Individual psychotherapy for adults contending with anxiety, depression, burnout, and relational challenges. Grounded in evidence, attentive to the whole person.

II

Educational Psychology

Assessment and intervention for children, adolescents, and young adults — addressing learning differences, attention, giftedness, and emotional wellbeing within educational contexts.

III

Vocational Counseling

Structured guidance for those at professional crossroads — clarifying strengths, values, and direction so that career choices serve the life you want to build.
